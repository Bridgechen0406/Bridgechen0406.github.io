    • [TITLE SEQUENCES OF THE FRENCH DISPATCH]


    This is a stop motion animation project, finished at 2023,07 The French Dispatch is one of my favorite movie.Hence, I decided to design a title sequences for it. With main characters walking along the background reveal some of details of their story.
    >TAP TO WATCH<

    Log Line: "Oh, so am I in a weekly? "

    In the movie, all of the main characters are "moving",whether riding bikes, hopping into past era, involing a student protest...and so on.
    Hence, I choose four main chracters and focus on their feet, in order not to reveal too much messages in this title sequence, which is a good preparation before the audience watch the movie.

    Characters: The cycling reporter / JKL Berensen who takes us to th past / The girl in the portest / The chief looking for something familiar in the foriegn land

    Atmosphere: A sence of seeking something familiar alone.

    All the characters are foreign in their own story, so I want to emphasize on their"difference", and their unique perspectives of the city they stayed in, so I decide the character feet will be gray scale before they gathered, and while they are walking, the background will be full of their "own colors, own perspectives".
